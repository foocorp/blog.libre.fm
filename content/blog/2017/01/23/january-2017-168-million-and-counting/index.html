January 2017: 168 million and&nbsp;counting

23 January, 2017

In a little over a year, we’ve added over 35 million more listens to Libre.fm.

As of right now (2:35pm on January 23rd) the following numbers are correct:

  • 168,516,592 listens.
  • 129,951,152 unique tracks.
  • 225,845 users.
  • 101,451 users active.
